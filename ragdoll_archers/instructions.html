🏹 RAGDOLL ARCHERS 🏹

🎮 CONTROLS

  • 🖱️ Move Mouse — Aim your bow
  • 🖱️ Hold Mouse Down — Draw back and charge your shot
  • 🖱️ Release Mouse — Fire your arrow

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Take down every ragdoll archer standing in your way. Land precise shots on your opponent's weak points to score kills and rack up the highest score you can.

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🏹 You've got unlimited arrows, so don't be afraid to fire.
  • 🎯 Aim carefully — a precise hit on a weak point does more damage than a rushed shot.
  • ❤️ Watch your health — getting hit by enemy arrows drains it fast.
  • 🔋 Keep an eye on your stamina — it drops with every shot you take.
  • 💀 Eliminate enemies to boost your kill count and climb your score.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 👀 Lead your shots — enemies move, so aim where they're going, not where they are.
  • ⚖️ Pace your shots so your stamina doesn't run out at a bad time.
  • 🎯 Weak point hits take enemies down faster than body shots.
  • 🛡️ Stay alert and dodge incoming arrows before they land.

🔄 RESTART

😅 If you get overwhelmed, press the Green Flag to restart.
