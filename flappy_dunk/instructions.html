🏀 Flappy Dunk - How to Play

Guide the basketball through as many hoops as possible!

Controls

  • Click the mouse or tap the screen to make the ball flap upward.
  • Release to let gravity pull the ball downward.

Objective

  • Pass through each hoop to score points.
  • Keep the ball in the air and avoid missing hoops.
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible.

Tips

  • Use short, controlled taps for better accuracy.
  • Time your flaps carefully as you approach each hoop.
  • Stay calm and maintain a steady rhythm.

Good luck and have fun dunking!
