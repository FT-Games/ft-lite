POLYTRACK INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to PolyTrack! Race through challenging tracks, beat your best times, and master jumps, loops, and shortcuts.

CONTROLS

W / Up Arrow - Accelerate
S / Down Arrow - Brake / Reverse
A / Left Arrow - Steer Left
D / Right Arrow - Steer Right
R - Restart Track
Space - Respawn at Checkpoint
C - Change Camera View

HOW TO PLAY

Drive through all checkpoints and reach the finish line as fast as possible.
Stay on the track and avoid crashes.
Use jumps, loops, and wall rides to maintain speed.
Restart instantly to improve your time and set new records.

TIPS

Keep your speed through corners.
Look for shortcuts and alternate routes.
Practice difficult sections repeatedly.
Watch your ghost replay to improve your racing line.

GOAL

Complete each track in the fastest time possible and challenge yourself to beat your personal best!
