Block Blast - Instructions

Objective

Place blocks on the board to complete full rows or columns. Completed rows and columns disappear, giving you points. Keep clearing space and aim for the highest score possible.

How to Play

  1. Drag one of the available block shapes onto the grid.
  2. Blocks cannot be rotated unless the game mode allows it.
  3. Fill an entire row or column to clear it.
  4. After placing all three available blocks, new blocks appear.
  5. The game ends when there is no room to place a remaining block.

Scoring

  • Clear rows and columns to earn points.
  • Clear multiple lines at once for bonus points.
  • Create combos by clearing lines on consecutive moves.

Tips for High Scores

  • Keep the center of the board open whenever possible.
  • Save large spaces for bigger block shapes.
  • Plan several moves ahead before placing blocks.
  • Avoid filling corners too early.
  • Look for opportunities to clear multiple lines at once.
Pro Tip: Always check all available block pieces before making a move. A placement that seems good now may prevent a larger piece from fitting later.
